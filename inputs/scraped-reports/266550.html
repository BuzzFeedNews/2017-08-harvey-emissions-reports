Regulated entity name PORT ARTHUR REFINERY Physical location
Regulated entity RN number RN100209451 City, County PORT ARTHUR, JEFFERSON
Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 08/29/2017 12:01AM
This is based on the: INITIAL REPORT Event ended: 08/30/2017 12:01AM
Cause On August 23, 2017, the Governor of the State of Texas issued a Proclamation declaring that Hurricane Harvey poses a threat of imminent disaster in 30 counties, including Jefferson County. The Port Arthur area received more than 44 inches of rain since Friday, August 25th with more than 26 inches falling between Tuesday, August 29th and Wednesday morning, August 30th. In response to increasing local flood conditions, Motiva began a controlled shutdown of the Port Arthur refinery in the early hours Wednesday morning, August 30. During the shutdown activities, the Refinery experienced process incidents that resulted in flaring.
Action taken Operations took immediate corrective actions to stabilize the units and reduce flaring in response to Hurricane Harvey. Return to service is contingent upon recession of flood waters in the area. Our priority remains the safety our employees and community.
Emissions estimation method Engineering Estimates.
